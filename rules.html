Rules


This is the rule page, in which you can find who counts as a viable member of the list, what the qualifications are for a level to be placed, and what is required in a viable completion. If some of these rules seem harsh, it's because this is for a friend group and we want it to stay that way.


Member viability


To be a viable member (aka to be able to submit levels/completions and to allow levels themed around YOU to be placed), you need to be part of the friend group server named the couch infinity unleashed AND you must have a Geometry Dash account (if I don't know your username, send it in the couch-list-gd-usernames channel and I'll notice it almost instantly). All eligible members are shown on the members/completions page.


Completion viability


To have a completion accepted, you must have a completion that follows these rules:


  • The completionist must be part of the friend group
  • The completion must be legitimate (duh)
  • A screenshot of the level complete screen as well as the level page (post-completion) must be provided
  • A cheat indicator must be visible at all times (exempt from this rule if you play vanilla GD)
  • A completion video is optional but highly recommended

If you don't follow these rules, your record will get DENIED.


Level submission viability


To have a level accepted and placed on the list, you must have the level follow these rules:


  • The creator, verifier and publisher must all be part of the friend group
  • The verification must be legitimate (duh)
  • The level must be listed on the servers (no unlisted levels)
  • The level must be at least 30 seconds long
  • The level must be harder than Can't Let Go (aka the hardest 1.0 RobTop level)
  • The level must have at least a minor role in the Discord server or must be dedicated to the Discord server or one of its eligible members in members/completions.
  • The level will get a botted showcase displayed until someone beats it with a verification video

If your level doesn't follow these rules, your level will get DENIED, however you are allowed to resubmit it if you fix the level.


Theming


The color scheme that you must use for every level themed around the list itself consists of the hex codes #BBBBFF, #8888FF and #4444FF (and of course full black/full white). Additionally, a level themed around the list itself must include :3/:D (or any other colon ASCII emoji)
